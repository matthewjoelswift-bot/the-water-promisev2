Where the water comes from

Every run pours a little water.

We don't drill wells. charity: water does — with a 20-year track record and public proof. Our job is to turn attention into money and send it their way, honestly and transparently.

The 100% model

charity: water sends 100% of public donations to the field — they even cover the card-processing fees — because their operating costs are funded separately by private backers and partners. That's why we can promise that the money we raise for water actually becomes water.

How a run becomes a cup

When you finish a run, the page refreshes its ads. Advertisers pay for those views. We take a share of that revenue and donate it — enough to fund at least one cup of clean water per run. The gap between what an ad earns and what a cup costs covers the small costs of running and growing this, so more people play and more water gets funded over time.

Transparency

We'll publish a live ledger of what's been donated and the receipts behind it. The counters here show cups and dollars given — the impact — and the completion proof (GPS and photos) comes straight from charity: water's own reporting.

Status: Prototype. We're setting up an official charity: water fundraising campaign so that giving flows directly to them and we can name them properly. Until that's live, no donations are being collected and every number here is illustrative.
