Get faster.
Give water.

Practice typing for a few minutes a day. Every run you finish refreshes an ad — and a share of that ad money funds a real cup of clean water through charity: water. You get faster; someone gets water. One run, one cup.

Actually get faster

A coach, not a timer

It watches the millisecond gaps between your keystrokes, finds the exact letters slowing you down, and drills them — keeping every round in the proven ~94%-accuracy sweet spot. Based on research across 136 million keystrokes.

Honest impact

One run = one cup

charity: water turns $1 into 1,100 liters — about 4,400 cups. So one cup costs a fraction of a cent, funded by the ad your run refreshed. A real number, not a metaphor.

Play anywhere

Desk or phone

On a keyboard you train speed. On your phone you solve a quick word puzzle. Same account, same streak, same well — the right game for the device you're on.
