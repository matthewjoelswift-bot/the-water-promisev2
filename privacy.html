Privacy Policy

This page explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, email us and we'll make it plainer.

Effective July 23, 2026 · The Water Promise is a project of The Little Tree Company, LLC.

Who we are

The Water Promise is a typing game that turns play into support for clean-water projects. It is operated by The Little Tree Company, LLC ("we," "us," "our"). If you have questions about this policy, email matt@littletreeco.com.

Information we collect

You can play the whole game without an account. If you choose to create one, we collect the email address and password you enter. Your password is handled by our sign-in provider and stored only in a securely hashed form — we never see it or store it in plain text.

As you play, we save your gameplay stats: your typing speed, accuracy, streaks, cups carried, and the display name you pick for the leaderboard. The display name you choose is shown publicly on the leaderboard; the rest stays tied to your account. We do not sell your personal information, and we don't ask for anything the game doesn't need.

Cookies and advertising

Advertising is what funds the free game, so ads on this site may use cookies. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time. Visit Google Ads Settings to manage the ads Google shows you, or www.aboutads.info/choices to opt out of many third-party vendors at once. If you're in the EU or UK, you can also manage your choices at www.youronlinechoices.eu. Opting out doesn't remove ads — it just makes them less tailored to you.

Analytics

We use basic, aggregate analytics to see how many people visit and which pages get used, so we can make the game better. This is counted at the whole-audience level and isn't used to identify you personally.

Services we link out to

Donations are handled directly by charity: water, and any payments are processed by Stripe. When you give or pay, that information goes straight to those organizations under their own privacy policies — it does not pass through us. We'd encourage you to read their policies before you give.

Children's privacy

The Water Promise is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has given us their information, email us and we'll delete it.

How your information is stored

Account and gameplay data is stored with our infrastructure providers using industry-standard safeguards. No method of storing or sending data over the internet is ever completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect what you share with us.

Your choices

You're always free to play without signing in. If you have an account and want your data removed, email matt@littletreeco.com and we'll take care of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the effective date at the top of this page.
